Alstone WPC Flooring- Raise your Expectations

WPC Flooring

Alstone WPC Flooring is considered the new generation of floor covering. Our products are an improvement of the previous generation of Luxury Vinyl tiles (LVT) & Wood polymer composite (WPC) that uses stone powder, polyvinyl chloride & special stabilizer that provides very stable composite material. Its wood-like texture & finishing provide unlimited styling & design concept for years to come.

Alstone WPC Flooring features extreme stability, high performance, completely waterproof, high-density core, indentation resistance & most importantly, it is recyclable. Its rigidity provides dimension stability & durability as planks do not shrink & expand to environmental conditions, unlike Vinyl tiles. Alstone WPC Flooring can be used for most applications. It is highly resistant to mold, termites attack & moist resistance. It has been installed & used successfully in kitchens, bathrooms, restaurants, gyms, and offices where heavy wear & tear is often the case. Most important, it is also fire resistant.

Alstone WPC Flooring looks & feels like real wood or stone depending on the design you may choose. As it is fully scratch-stain resistant, tedious polishing is not required for maintenance. Alstone WPC Flooring is easy to install & can be handled without a professional installer. Our products feature a click lock system that allows quick “Do-It-Yourself” installation without the mess of nails and glue. The click system firmly secures every piece with minimal gaps in between.

When buying or specifying floorings, it is vital to understand the product’s suitability for its intended application or usage class. To understand this the international standards have created an Abrasion Criteria or AC Rating. The AC RATING is based on the flooring’s resistance to stresses like burns, scratches, stains, and impact. They even look at factors like how the weight of furniture affects the product’s performance. Failing one aspect of the testing can cause a line to receive failing marks.
