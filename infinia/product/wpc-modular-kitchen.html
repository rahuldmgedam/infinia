What is WPC Material?

WPC, which stands for Wood Plastic Composite, is a game changer in kitchen design. This novel material blends the natural beauty of wood with the durability and adaptability of plastic. WPC combines wood fibres or sawdust with thermoplastic resins to form a strong material ideal for culinary uses.
